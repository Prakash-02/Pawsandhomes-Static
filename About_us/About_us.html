About Us

At Paws&Homes, our mission is to find loving homes for pets in need. We believe every animal deserves a chance to be a part of a family, and we're dedicated to making that happen through our adoption services.

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome animals in need. We work tirelessly to provide these pets with a second chance at a happy, healthy life by connecting them with caring adopters.

Meet Our Team

Alice Johnson

Alice is our lead veterinarian with over 10 years of experience in animal care. She ensures all our pets are healthy and happy.

Bob Smith

Bob is our adoption coordinator. He helps match pets with their perfect forever homes and provides support throughout the adoption process.

Catherine Lee

Catherine is our animal behaviorist. She works with pets to address any behavioral issues and helps them adjust to their new homes.
